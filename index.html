A heads-up before you play

This game sends a small amount of anonymous gameplay data — things like which zones you clear, how long runs take, and where players get stuck. It helps me tune the balance.

What is sent: a random session ID (re-used only within your own browser), game version, progression milestones, workforce allocation, and resource counts.

What is never sent: your name, email, IP address, location, any account identifier, or any data that could identify you personally. There are no third-party trackers and no advertising.

You can turn it off at any time: click the ⚙ gear icon in the top-right and uncheck Send anonymous gameplay stats.
